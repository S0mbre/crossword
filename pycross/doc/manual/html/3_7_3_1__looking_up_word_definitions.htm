3.7.3.1. Looking up word definitions

 
If you are unsure about the meaning of a word, you can look it up in the online dictionary or Google using the inbuilt online search feature.
 
To look up the selected word, select the Edit > Lookup word menu, or press the button on the Toolbar, or use the Ctrl + L hotkey. The Word Lookup dialog will show up:
 
Fig. 3.7.3.1.1. Word Lookup dialog

The word being looked up

The selected word in the grid whose meaning you are looking up.
Top

Search language

The language used to search the word's meaning. Default is English (en).
This setting affects the selection of the online dictionary (Merriam-Webster for English, Yandex for other languages) and some relevant Google search configuration.
Top

Online dictionary results

Switch to the dictionary tab to show results from the online dictionary (Merriam-Webster or Yandex).
Top

Google results

Switch to the Google tab to show results retrieved by Google.
Top

List of results

Choose the specific page from the Google results or specific meaning from the dictionary.

Result field

This field shows a brief annotation of the selected result. The field is read-only, but you can copy any text to the clipboard.

Link to full result

Top

Cancel button

OK button
